Our Project

Project Atlas aims to develop an inexpensive lower-limb assistive exoskeleton which would improve the ease of human bipedal movement. The goals of the device are to delay the onset of fatigue in people traveling by foot, mitigating any pain or disability caused by muscle strains, or past injuries.

Join Us!

Project Atlas current design stays true to the term “biomechatronics”. The project incorporates aspects of mechanical, electrical, and software design. A major benefit of working with the Atlas team is the exposure to all aspects of design that the project provides. There is always work to be done, so don't hesitate! Join us!
