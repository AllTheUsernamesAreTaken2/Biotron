Who Are We?

A group of passionate students who are interested in learning and applying the latest technologies regarding wearable powered prostheses. We share a variety of different backgrounds, experience levels, and skills, but we are all here to build some amazing new prosthetic arms!

Our Projects

Originally, our main goal was to compete in the 2016 Cybathlon Powered Arm Prosthesis Race, held in Zurich, Switzerland. However, our current aims are more focused on exploring the field of prosthetic research and attempting to recreate simple versions of industry-level prototypes. This will help us gain more background knowledge, as well as hands-on experience with circuits, programming, and design. Explore our current projects below!
